## 🎨 Creative Engine

The Creative Engine is a new mode designed for artistic freedom and experimentation, separate from the competitive game rules.

### Key Features
1.  **Freeform Placement**: Place stones and laser sources anywhere on the grid without energy costs or turn limits.
2.  **Custom Properties**: 
    - **Color**: Assign any RGB color to stones and lasers.
    - **Bloom & Glow**: Adjust the intensity of lighting effects.
    - **Textures**: Load custom images onto stones.
3.  **New Objects**:
    - **Laser Source**: A draggable emitter that can shoot in any direction.
    - **STOP Stone**: A new stone type that absorbs laser beams.
4.  **Recording**: The engine automatically records your creation process, allowing for step-by-step replay.
5.  **High-Res Export**: Render your scene at 2x resolution for high-quality screenshots.

### Controls
- **Select Tool**: Click objects to edit their properties in the side panel.
- **Laser Tool**: Drag on the board to create a configured laser source.
- **Save/Load**: Save your scene as a `.gls` file to continue working later.

### Advanced Features
6. **Animation System**:
    - **Stone Rotation**: Toggle automatic rotation for stones regardless of their type.
    - **Speed Control**: Fine-tune rotation speed (degrees per frame) to create dynamic kinetic art.
7. **Environment Control**:
    - **Sky Gradient**: Customize the background with dual-color gradients (Top/Bottom) for sunset or sci-fi aesthetics.
    - **Background Textures**: Load custom images to serve as the board background.
    - **Atmosphere**: Apply an atmospheric overlay with adjustable density (fog) and color to add depth.
    - **Board Settings**: Access these global settings immediately by clicking on any empty space on the board.
